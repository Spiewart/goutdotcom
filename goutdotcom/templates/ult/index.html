Urate-lowering therapy (ULT)

Urate-lowering therapy is the cornerstone of chronic management of gout. For people with persistent gout, it can literally be life-changing.

Not everyone who has gout needs chronic management and there is some nuance to starting ULT, particularly in the first 12 months of therapy.
