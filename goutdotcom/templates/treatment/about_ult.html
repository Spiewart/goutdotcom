Urate-lowering therapy

Urate-lowering therapy (ULT) is the cornerstone of gout prevention. Uric acid gets in the body from our diet and our body's own production of it. It is eliminated by the kidneys. The two most common (and effective)ULT therapies are allopurinol and febuxostat (Uloric). They work by preventing our body's production of uric acid.
