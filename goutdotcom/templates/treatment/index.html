Gout Treatment

Managing gout is broken down into two parts:


1. Managing the symptoms of your gout flares.


2. Long-term treatment to prevent and even eliminate gout flares.

Not everyone needs long-term treatment of their gout, but most who do take daily medication to prevent gout no longer have to worry about flares.
